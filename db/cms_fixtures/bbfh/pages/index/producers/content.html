Our Producers

We source a wide range of wonderful fresh produce from across West Gippsland and we're proud to support the truly outstanding farmers and beautiful farms where it is grown. Most of them are very experienced organic growers, who are dedicated stewards of their land, protecting soil health, biodiversity and water quality and maintaining high standards of animal welfare and crop production methods without the use of chemical inputs.

A major purpose of the Baw Baw Food Hub is to support and mentor a greater quantity and variety of these ecological farming systems in our region.

Elderslie Organics

The Wallace family are dairy farmers and free range egg producers at Elliinbank, south of Warragul. Their flocks of hens nest and sleep in caravans which follow the cows around the farm. The "girls" contribute to soil health and fertility and produce fantastic fresh eggs. The family are part of the Organic Dairy Farmers of Australia Co-op, whose milk goes into the making of the cheddar and parmesan cheeses and butter which we offer each week.
http://www.elderslieorganics.com.au


Piedmont Organic Farm

Liz Clay and Wally Brown farm at Noojee, where their small market garden overlooks the upper Latrobe River to beautiful mountain ash forests. They grow a broad mix of vegetables, specialising in potatoes, as well as raspberries, strawberries and other fruits. Liz is part of the Baw Baw Food Hub team, has been involved with organics for many years, including being a member of the world board of the International Federation of Organic Farming Movements.

Cafresco  

Maurie and Maria Cafra and their band of workers grow an abundance of beautiful NASAA certified organic vegetables at Koo Wee Rup. As very experienced and innovative organic growers, they have developed a model of sustainable food production. Compost processed on-farm continually contributes to healthy soil life and nutritious produce. Tomatoes, broccoli, asparagus, peas, beans, eggplant, capsicums, kale, cabbage, onions, sweet corn and pumpkins are some of the lines from Cafresco to be found in our vegetable boxes.

Peninsula Fresh

Wayne and Tash, Sue and Rod are the people behind Peninsula Fresh Organics, supplying a range of co-ops and selling at Farmer's Markets. To extend the range of produce available across the seasons, they are also farming at Barham, on the Murray River. Some of the vegetables from Peninsula Fresh that appear in our veggie boxes are Kale, lettuce, silver beet, beetroot, fennel, cabbage, carrots, radish, celery, parsley, kohlrabi and bok choi.
ACO certified organic.
http://www.peninsulafresh.com

Sunny Creek Organic Berry Farm

This very beautiful, complex and productive small farm at Childers is the creation of Phil Rowe and Cathie Taylor. Berries include boysenberries, blackberries, strawberries and raspberries of all colours. Products available to our customers include fresh and frozen berries and delicious jams, all NASAA certified organic.
http://www.sunnycreekorganic.com.au

Willowgrove Natural Home Garden produce 

Leni and Paul grow a range of vegetables and vegetable seedlings on their small farm. They are continually working on improving their soil fertility and structure and production techniques. We have included their tomatoes, red capsicums, book choi and pumpkins in our veggie boxes.

Blue Sky Organics

The Watts family produce five different varieties of outstanding organic garlic at their farm at Murrindal, near Buchan. OGA certified organic.

Hazeldean Forest Farm

The Alexander family specializes in NASAA certified organic apples, but also produces plums, persimmons and chestnuts and nashis in season. Their farm at Ellinbank features established orchards and extensive native plantings in a mature ecological production system.
http://hazeldeanforestfarm.com


Cannibal Creek Bakehouse
