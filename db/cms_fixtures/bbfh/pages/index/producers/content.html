Our Producers

We source produce from many different producers across West Gippsland. This is a short overview of who all the talented growers are that create all of the fantastic food that we offer!


Elderslie Organics

The Wallace family are dairy farmers and free range egg producers. Their flocks of hens follow the cows around the farm, contributing to soil health and fertility and producing fantastic fresh eggs. The family are part of the Organic Dairy Farmers of Australia Co-op, whose milk goes into the making of the cheddar and parmesan cheeses and butter which we offer each week.

Piedmont Organic Farm

Liz and Wally farm at Noojee, where they grow a broad mix of vegetables, specialising in potatoes. 

Cafresco  

Maurie and Maria Cafra grow beautiful vegetables at Koo Wee Rup. Compost produced on-farm contributes to healthy soil life and nutritious produce. We include their tomatoes, broccoli, asparagus, eggplant, capsicums, onions and pumpkins in our vegetable boxes.

Peninsula Fresh

Vegetables from Peninsula Fresh that appear in our veggie boxes include Kale, lettuce, beetroot, fennel, cabbage, carrots, parsley and bok choi.

Sunny Creek Organic Berry Farm

is a very beautiful, complex and productive small farm at Childers. Berries include boysenberries, blackberries, strawberries and raspberries of all colours. Products available to our customers include fresh and frozen berries and delicious jams, all NASAA certified organic.

Willowgrove Natural Home Garden produce 

We have included their tomatoes, red capsicums and pumpkins in our veggie boxes.

Blue Sky Organics

The Watts family produce a range of varieties of outstanding organic garlic at their farm at Murrindal, near Buchan. 

Hazeldean Forest Farm

specializes in NASAA certified organic apples, but also produces plums, persimmons and chestnuts, in season.
