Local + Organic Vegetable Boxes

At the heart of our Food Hub is the Baw Baw Fresh Organics veggie box scheme. Each week, we source and pack the best local produce we can find, from our group of ecological farmers. The boxes offer fantastic convenience, variety, quality, value and outstanding freshness. For more information about how much good this new way of sourcing food can do, read “What are the benefits?”(link)

How it works

Our subscribers sign up for a 12-week season of vegetable boxes, and are offered a 5% discount for paying upfront. We offer three sizes of boxes, to cater for different households:
    • a larger box of 12-15 vegetables for $45
    • a standard box with 10 -12 vegetables for $30
    • or a small bag of 8-10 lines for $20, suitable for single-person households
It is possible to change box sizes by contacting us by the Sunday evening before pick-up.

Extras

Through our online shop (open Wednesday through Saturday each week), we also offer a wide and ever-expanding range of other local organic foods, including cheese and butter from, yoghurt and butter, berries, apples and other fruit, eggs, breads, pork and beef.

Payment

Can be made by EFT into our bank account, or in cash at the pick-up. We ask for two weeks payment at the start of the season, so that members have paid for their box before we pick produce and pack it. There is no charge for the final box of the season.

Pick-up

Pick-up is each Tuesday afternoon, in our cosy packing space at the rear of 34 Mason Street, Warragul, between 4 and 6pm.During this time, our members:
  • Return their empty box from the previous week
  • Collect their new veggie box and the other goods they have ordered online, such cheeses, bread and eggs
  • Choose from a range of other produce for sale, including seasonal fruit
  • Have the opportunity to use the Swap Box to change one or two items for an alternative item that they would prefer
  • Pay, if EFT payment has not already been made

No Shows

If you are not able to make it to the pick-up:
  1. Organise for a friend or family member to collect your box, or gift it to them
  2. If this can’t be arranged, let us know as early as possible and we will try to place it with someone looking to try out our boxes
  3. If we don’t hear from you, or manage to place your box with someone else, we will give it away

Trial boxes

We do offer trial boxes which are can be purchased each week between Wednesday and Saturday via our online store. Trial box numbers are generally limited and so are subject to availability.
