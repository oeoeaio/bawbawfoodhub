Baw Baw Food Hub is a not-for-profit group based in Warragul that sources organic, natural and local produce for people in the Baw Baw area of West Gippsland.

We pack boxes of fresh, local and organic produce for our customers each week. We also have a great range of local organic produce and extras that can be purchased through our online store.

We pack veggie boxes every Tuesday. Unless you are signed up for the current season, you will need to order a box and/or any extras by 13:00 on Sunday. The autumn season runs up until Tuesday 26 May 2015.

Sign up above for the autumn season and be sure to get a box prepared for you each week. Or you can purchase a box on a casual basis, as you need it, via the online store.  Find out more about our veggie boxes....
