Summer is here!

The veggies are in plentiful supply again, and we're very excited to be to packing our boxes of local, fresh vegetables on a weekly basis!

As usual, the pickup will be at the rear of 34 Mason Street, Warragul, between 4pm and 6pm on Tuesdays. For more information about how the boxes work and what to expect, have a look at our overview.

We pack boxes to order, and so we need some lead time to harvest produce and place orders with our other local growers. To receive a box on Tuesday, you will need to sign up before midday on the previous Sunday. 

Subscriptions are open to everyone, so feel free so invite your friends and family members to sign up for a box.

See you at the pickup!
