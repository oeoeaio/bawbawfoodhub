Signup for Summer

Welcome to summer! We'll be packing veggie boxes weekly throughout this season, except for a two week break over Christmas and new years, meaning that there will be no veggie boxes on December 29th or January 5th.

Pickup will be from our venue at Unit 4, 133 North Road, Warragul, between 3pm and 6pm on Tuesdays. For more information about how the boxes work and what to expect, have a look at our overview.

We pack boxes to order, and so we need some lead time to harvest produce and place orders with our other local growers. To receive a box on Tuesday, you will need to sign up before midday on the previous Sunday. 

Subscriptions are open to everyone, so feel free so invite your friends and family members to sign up for a box.

If you aren't able to commit to collecting a box each week, feel free to order the "flexible" version of your desired box size from our online store as and when you want one. Please note however that these attract a $3.00 administration surcharge.

See you at the pickup!
