Our Range of Dried Goods and Extras

In addition to our weekly offering of fresh vegetables, fruit and veggie boxes, Baw Baw Food Hub now stocks a wide range of dried and other items that are for purchase by members and casual customers. The main categories listed on this page are:
  • Dried and bulk goods
  • Dairy
  • Perishable and fresh food
  • Jams and preserves

The following sections describe the range of items normally available. Most items are organic and the source and certification are usually listed. Please note that the source of items and certification may change from time to time.

The follow abbreviations are used for organic certification:
NASAA - National Associations for Sustainable Agriculture, Australia
Biodymanic (Demeter)
ACO - Australia Certified Organic
Aus-qual - Export organic certification

Dried and bulk goods

The Baw Baw Food Hub stocks the range of dried and bulk foods as listed below. Unless otherwise noted, these items are available in bulk - you can purchase as little or as much as you want. The great thing about this approach is the we are able to avoid excess packaging so please make sure you bring your own containers or bags. Paper bags are available at the hub. At this stage bulk items are ONLY available for purchase at the food hub (they cannot be ordered on-line). All items are produce of Australia.
