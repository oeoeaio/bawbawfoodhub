Randall Organic Rice

Our organic rice is grown by the Randall family, at Murrami, near Leeton, in southern NSW. We are able to maintain a very reasonable price, by dealing directly with the Randall's and through a handy back-load from the Melbourne Farmer's Markets (thanks, Steve!). We stock Doongarra (Low G.I.), Koshihikari (brown and white), long grain, plus rice crackers and rice flours.
