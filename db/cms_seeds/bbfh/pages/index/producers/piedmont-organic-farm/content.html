Piedmont Organic Farm

Liz Clay and Wally Brown farm at Noojee, where their small market garden overlooks the upper Latrobe River to beautiful mountain ash forests. They grow a broad mix of vegetables, specialising in potatoes, as well as raspberries, strawberries and other fruits. Liz is part of the Baw Baw Food Hub team, has been involved with organics for many years, including being a member of the world board of the International Federation of Organic Farming Movements.
