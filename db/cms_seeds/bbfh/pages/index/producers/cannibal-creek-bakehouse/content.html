Cannibal Creek Bakehouse

Each week, a selection of the wonderful sourdough breads from Cannibal Creek Bakehouse are offered through our online shop. Using NASAA certified organic flour and wild ferments, James and his team bake a delicious range of breads and other baked goods in the the restored wood-fired oven at Garfield.
