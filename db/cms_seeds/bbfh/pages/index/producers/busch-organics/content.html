Busch Organics

Busch Organics is a family run Australian Certified Organic farm on the Mitchell River Flats near Bairnsdale. Quality of produce, soil health and sustainable practice are important to Kane and Matthew, their father Chris and grandfather Lex. Broccoli, sweetcorn, parsnips, beans and beetroot are some of the veggies they produce.
