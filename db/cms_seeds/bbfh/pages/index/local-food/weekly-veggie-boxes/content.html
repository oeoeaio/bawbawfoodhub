Local + Organic Vegetable Boxes

At the heart of our Food Hub is the Baw Baw Fresh Organics veggie box scheme. Each week, we source and pack the best local produce we can find, from our group of ecological farmers. The boxes offer fantastic convenience, variety, quality, value and outstanding freshness. For more on why we think local and organic are important, have a look at our principals and values.

How it works

Our subscribers sign up for a three-month season of vegetable boxes. We offer three different box sizes, to cater for different households:
    • a larger box of 12-15 vegetables for $47
    • a standard box with 10 -12 vegetables for $32
    • or a small bag of 8-10 lines for $22, suitable for single-person households
It is possible to change box sizes by contacting us by the Sunday evening before pick-up. You can sign up for the remaining weeks in the season at any time. Flexible boxes can be ordered from week to week for an additional $3 per box.

Extras

Through our online shop (open Wednesday through to Sunday at 13:00 each week), we also offer a wide and ever-expanding range of other local organic foods, including cheese, yoghurt and butter, berries, apples and other fruit, eggs, pork and beef.

Payment

Payments for boxes and extras can be made by EFT into our bank account, or in cash at the pick-up. Customers paying for all boxes to the end of the current season upfront are offered a 5% discount. If you are ordering a casual or trial box, you will need to pre-pay on line for the box and any extras.

Pick-up

Pick-up is each Tuesday afternoon, in our cosy packing space at Unit 4, 133 North Road, Warragul, between 3pm and 6pm. During this time, our members:
  • Return their empty box from the previous week.
  • Collect their new veggie box and the other goods they have ordered online, such cheeses, yoghurt and eggs.
  • Choose from a range of other produce for sale, including seasonal fruit
  • Have the opportunity to use the Swap Box to change one or two items for an alternative item that they would prefer.
  • Pay, if EFT payment has not already been made.

No Shows

If you are not able to make it to the pick-up:
  1. Please organise for a friend or family member to collect your box, or gift it to them.
  2. If this can’t be arranged, let us know as early as possible and we will try to find a home for your box.
  3. If we don’t hear from you, or if we don't manage to find a home for your box, it is donated to the Baw Baw Combined Churches Food Relief on Wednesday. You will be expected to pay for your box unless you cancel it before 13:00 on the Sunday before.


Flexible boxes

For those who require flexibility, and therefore cannot commit to collecting a veggie box on weekly basis, we do offer 'flexible' boxes which can be order from our online shop as required. Note that these attract an additional administration fee.


Trial boxes

We also offer trial boxes which are can be purchased each week between Wednesday and Sunday via our online shop. Trial box numbers are generally limited and so are subject to availability.

How do I join?

To become a member of Baw Baw Fresh Organics, fill out the online sign-up form for the current season here. This guarantees you a box for each week of the remainder of the season, allows you to order through our online shop, and to participate in various offers and events throughout the year. At the end of the season, places for the new season will be offered to existing subscribers first.
